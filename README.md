# [Katzu- Landing Page]((https://katzu.work))

## About

Katzu is a platform dedicated to helping artists, designers, musicians, and creators build their personal brand online. 

The landing page is minimal, clean, and designed to highlight the platform’s mission: helping artists showcase their work, communicate their identity, and connect with opportunities.
